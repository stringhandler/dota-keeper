Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Dota Keeper.

What does Dota Keeper do?

Dota Keeper tracks your Dota 2 match history and measures your performance against goals you define. Set a target — like "100 last hits by 10 minutes" or "die fewer than 5 times" — and Dota Keeper shows how often you're hitting it across your recent games.

How does it get my match data?

Dota Keeper fetches your match history from OpenDota using your Steam ID. No login required — just enter your Steam ID in the settings and click sync.

What kinds of goals can I set?

You can set goals for last hits, denies, net worth, kills, level, and item timing — all filterable by hero and game mode (Ranked, Turbo, or Any). Goals are designed to be hit roughly 75% of the time: challenging enough to matter, achievable enough to stay motivating.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your machine in a SQLite database. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Dota Keeper contains no ads, no tracking, and no accounts.

Is it free?

Completely free, forever. Dota Keeper is open source under the Apache 2.0 license. You can view, fork, or contribute to the code on GitHub.

What platforms does it run on?

Windows, macOS, and Linux. Download the installer for your platform from the download section above.
