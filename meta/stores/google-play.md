## Full Description
_(4000 characters max)_

```
Level up your Dota 2 performance — without giving up your privacy.

Dota Keeper automatically syncs your match history via OpenDota and helps you set meaningful personal goals, track your progress, and stay mentally sharp. Everything runs locally on your device. No account required. No cloud. No ads. Free forever.

──────────────────────────────
MATCH TRACKING
──────────────────────────────
• Automatically fetch your full match history using your Steam ID
• View detailed match breakdowns: KDA, GPM, XPM, net worth, heroes played, and more
• Filter matches by hero, role, win/loss, and date range
• Backfill historical matches to build a complete picture of your game

──────────────────────────────
CUSTOM PERFORMANCE GOALS
──────────────────────────────
• Create goals that match your playstyle — CS per minute, KDA, GPM, win rate by hero, deaths per game, and more
• Track goal progress across recent matches with clear pass/fail indicators
• Goals are calibrated to be challenging but achievable (~75% success rate)
• Get smart hero-specific goal suggestions based on your stats

──────────────────────────────
WEEKLY & DAILY CHALLENGES
──────────────────────────────
• Accept personalised weekly challenges generated from your recent performance
• Reroll or skip challenges that don't fit your schedule
• Track challenge history and streaks over time

──────────────────────────────
PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS
──────────────────────────────
• Hero-by-hero performance breakdowns with win rates and stat distributions
• Identify your strongest and weakest heroes at a glance
• Trend analysis over time to see if you're improving

──────────────────────────────
MENTAL HEALTH & TILT TRACKING
──────────────────────────────
• Optional post-game mood check-ins to track emotional state over time
• Tilt assessment based on your recent match patterns and mood history
• Suggestions for when to take a break or switch heroes

──────────────────────────────
PRIVACY FIRST
──────────────────────────────
• All data stored locally on your device — no servers, no cloud, no tracking
• No account needed — just your Steam ID
• Open source (Apache 2.0) — inspect every line of code on GitHub

Dota Keeper is not affiliated with or endorsed by Valve Corporation.
Dota 2 is a registered trademark of Valve Corporation.
```
