## Description
_(4000 characters max)_

```
Level up your Dota 2 performance — without giving up your privacy.

Dota Keeper automatically syncs your match history and helps you set meaningful personal goals, track your progress, and stay mentally sharp. Everything runs locally on your device. No account required. No cloud. No ads. Free forever.


MATCH TRACKING

Automatically fetch your full match history using your Steam ID. View detailed breakdowns of every game — KDA, GPM, XPM, net worth, heroes played, and more. Filter by hero, role, win/loss, or date. Backfill historical matches to build a complete picture of your performance.


CUSTOM PERFORMANCE GOALS

Create goals that match your playstyle — last hits per minute, KDA ratio, GPM targets, win rate on specific heroes, deaths per game, and more. Goals are calibrated to be challenging but achievable, aiming for around a 75% success rate so you stay motivated without feeling stuck. Get hero-specific goal suggestions automatically generated from your stats.


WEEKLY & DAILY CHALLENGES

Accept personalised weekly challenges generated from your recent performance. Reroll or skip challenges that don't suit you. Track your challenge history and see how your consistency improves over time.


PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

Hero-by-hero breakdowns with win rates and stat distributions. Identify your strongest heroes and spot patterns across roles. Visualise your performance trends over time.


MENTAL HEALTH & TILT TRACKING

Dota can be stressful. Dota Keeper includes optional post-game mood check-ins so you can track how you're feeling over time. The tilt assessment looks at your recent match patterns and mood history and suggests when it might be time to take a break — before a loss streak turns into a bad night.


PRIVACY FIRST

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers — because we don't have any. No account registration. No subscriptions. No ads. Just open-source software built for players, by players.

Open source under Apache 2.0 — view every line of code at github.com/stringhandler/dota-keeper.


Dota Keeper is not affiliated with or endorsed by Valve Corporation. Dota 2 is a registered trademark of Valve Corporation.
