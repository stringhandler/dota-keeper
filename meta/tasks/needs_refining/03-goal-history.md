
The design philosophy is that you should be tracking the goal over time. You should be able to see the goal's history and how well you were achieving it over time. 

For example, the goal of 50 last hits at 10 minutes. It should show, over time how you were getting 30 last hits on average in 80% of your games. then it when to 35%, and you weren't hitting it, but then you improved and were hitting it. the aim is to slowly increase the user's achievement in that goal over time. and this should be measure.d
